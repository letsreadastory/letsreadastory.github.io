..The Stag And His Reflection..



Once a stag was drinking water from a spring.

He saw his reflection in the clear water.

He felt happy that he looked so handsome!

The stag felt proud of his antlers.

But then the stag saw his legs and became sad.

He thought, 'Oh! My legs are so thin and ugly!'.

'I wish God had given me beautiful legs too!'

The stag felt ashamed of his legs.

Suddenly, he heard a sound from the trees.

He became alert and turned towards it.

There stood a tiger behind the tree!

The stag saw him and began to run! He ran fast on his ugly legs.

But, his big beautiful antlers got stuck in a bush.

The tiger soon caught him!

The stag's ugly legs were helpful, but his beautiful antlers were not.


..The end..


...Moral of the story!...


Value things for their usefulness, not for their looks.
